Layered-Learning™ approach

At DePaul, education goes beyond books and lectures. Guided by a student-first philosophy, our Layered-Learning™ approach connects classroom lessons with project-based learning and service learning. You won’t just learn about the world, you’ll engage with it and grow professionally.

Classroom learning

With a 17:1 student-to-faculty ratio, DePaul’s small class sizes mean more personalized face time with your professors and academic advisors. You’ll learn from professors who are experts in their fields, infusing innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge research, and valuable professional networks directly into the classroom. At DePaul, we don’t rely primarily on teaching assistants. Your professors lead your classes, bringing their knowledge directly to you.

Project-based learning

Chicago is your lab, activated by DePaul’s professors, whose relationships across the city open doors to real-world, competitive opportunities. Collaborate with local organizations, lead projects that matter, and graduate with a portfolio that sets you apart.

Service
learning

Make an impact while earning your degree. Through the Steans Center, DePaul students engage in more than 3,500 service hours with over 250 community organizations.
